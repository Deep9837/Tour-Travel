Yamunotri

Yamunotri Dham, the first Dham en route the yatra, is situated at Yamunotri- where the sacred river the Yamuna originates.

Gangotri

Dedicated to Goddess Ganga, Gangothri Dham is the second of the four temples at the Chota Char Dham Route. Gangothri Dham is the birthplace of the River Ganga.

Kedarnath

The Kedarnath Dham is known to be one of the five spots believed to be where Lord Shiva resides. In the Vedas and Puranas, the word ‘Kedar’ refers to Lord Shiva.

Badrinath

Badrinath is sacred to Lord Vishnu, particularly in Vishnu's dual form of Nara-Narayana. Badrinath temple was re-established by Adi Shankracharya in the 8th century as part of his mission to enliven Hinduism.
